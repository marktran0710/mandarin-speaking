《捷運站在哪裡？》Where Is the MRT Station?
Situation: A student is running late for class and stops a stranger on the street to ask for directions to the MRT station.

═══════════════════
EASY (A1)
═══════════════════

[Turn 1 — 路上 On the street, morning; 承翰 checks the time, looking lost]
Narrator：承翰要去學校。他不知道捷運站在哪裡。
Chénghàn yào qù xuéxiào. Tā bù zhīdào jiéyùnzhàn zài nǎlǐ.
Chenghan needs to go to school. He doesn't know where the MRT station is.

[Turn 2 — 承翰 stops a passerby, 婉婷, on the sidewalk]
承翰：請問，捷運站在哪裡？
Qǐngwèn, jiéyùnzhàn zài nǎlǐ?
Excuse me, where is the MRT station?

[Turn 3 — 婉婷 points down the street]
婉婷：捷運站在那裡。
Jiéyùnzhàn zài nàlǐ.
The MRT station is over there.

[Turn 4 — 承翰 looks at the distance, a little worried]
承翰：遠嗎？
Yuǎn ma?
Is it far?

[Turn 5 — 婉婷 shakes her head, smiles]
婉婷：不遠，很近。
Bù yuǎn, hěn jìn.
Not far, very close.

[Turn 6 — 承翰 smiles with relief, walks off toward the station]
Narrator：承翰說謝謝。他走去捷運站。他很高興。
Chénghàn shuō xièxie. Tā zǒu qù jiéyùnzhàn. Tā hěn gāoxìng.
Chenghan says thank you. He walks to the MRT station. He is happy.

Vocabulary
1  要      yào        Aux  want to; need to
2  去      qù         V    to go
3  學校    xuéxiào    N    school
4  知道    zhīdào     V    to know
5  捷運站  jiéyùnzhàn N    MRT station
6  哪裡    nǎlǐ       N    where
7  請問    qǐngwèn    Ph   excuse me (polite opener)
8  在      zài        Vst  to be located at
9  那裡    nàlǐ       N    there
10 遠      yuǎn       Vs   far
11 嗎      ma         Ptc  question particle
12 近      jìn        Vs   close; near
13 謝謝    xièxie     V/Ph thank you
14 走      zǒu        V    to walk
15 高興    gāoxìng    Vs   happy

Key Phrases
1  X 在哪裡？   —  請問，捷運站在哪裡？   —  Ask where a place is; swap in any noun.
2  不 + Vs      —  不遠，很近。            —  Negate a simple adjective (far/near, big/small) with 不.
3  很 + Vs      —  他很高興。              —  Attach 很 before an adjective to describe a feeling — no 是 needed.

═══════════════════
MEDIUM (A1-A2)
═══════════════════

[Turn 1 — 路上 On the street, early morning; 承翰 hurries along checking his phone]
Narrator：今天承翰要去學校上課，可是他不知道捷運站在哪裡，心裡很緊張。
Jīntiān Chénghàn yào qù xuéxiào shàngkè, kěshì tā bù zhīdào jiéyùnzhàn zài nǎlǐ, xīnlǐ hěn jǐnzhāng.
Today Chenghan needs to go to school for class, but he doesn't know where the MRT station is, and he feels very nervous.

[Turn 2 — 承翰 stops 婉婷 on the sidewalk]
承翰：不好意思，請問捷運站怎麼走？
Bùhǎoyìsi, qǐngwèn jiéyùnzhàn zěnme zǒu?
Excuse me, how do I get to the MRT station?

[Turn 3 — 婉婷 points ahead down the road]
婉婷：你往前走，咖啡店旁邊就是捷運站。
Nǐ wǎng qián zǒu, kāfēidiàn pángbiān jiù shì jiéyùnzhàn.
Walk straight ahead — the MRT station is right beside the coffee shop.

[Turn 4 — 承翰 checks his watch, anxious]
承翰：要走多久？我沒有很多時間。
Yào zǒu duōjiǔ? Wǒ méiyǒu hěn duō shíjiān.
How long does it take to walk? I don't have much time.

[Turn 5 — 婉婷 smiles reassuringly]
婉婷：不會很久，大概五分鐘，你可以走快一點。
Bú huì hěn jiǔ, dàgài wǔ fēnzhōng, nǐ kěyǐ zǒu kuài yìdiǎn.
It won't take long, about five minutes — you can walk a bit faster.

[Turn 6 — 承翰 smiles, hurries off]
Narrator：承翰謝謝婉婷，因為她的幫忙，他準時到學校。
Chénghàn xièxie Wǎntíng, yīnwèi tā de bāngmáng, tā zhǔnshí dào xuéxiào.
Chenghan thanks Wanting; because of her help, he arrives at school on time.

Key Phrases
1  X 怎麼走？        —  請問捷運站怎麼走？          —  Ask for directions to a place.
2  要 V 多久？        —  要走多久？                  —  Ask how long an activity takes.
3  因為 X，Y          —  因為她的幫忙，他準時到學校。 —  Give a reason before its result.

═══════════════════
HARD (A2)
═══════════════════

[Turn 1 — 路上 On the street; 承翰 rushes along, checking his phone anxiously]
Narrator：承翰今天起晚了，他要趕快去學校，可是他從來沒走過這條路，心裡很緊張。
Chénghàn jīntiān qǐ wǎn le, tā yào gǎnkuài qù xuéxiào, kěshì tā cónglái méi zǒuguò zhè tiáo lù, xīnlǐ hěn jǐnzhāng.
Chenghan got up late today; he needs to hurry to school, but he's never walked this road before, so he feels very nervous.

[Turn 2 — 承翰 stops 婉婷, slightly out of breath]
承翰：不好意思，請問一下，去捷運站是不是要一直往前走？
Bùhǎoyìsi, qǐng wèn yíxià, qù jiéyùnzhàn shì bú shì yào yìzhí wǎng qián zǒu?
Excuse me, may I ask — to get to the MRT station, do I need to keep walking straight ahead?

[Turn 3 — 婉婷 points toward a traffic light in the distance]
婉婷：對，你一直走，走到紅綠燈右轉，比走這條巷子快多了。
Duì, nǐ yìzhí zǒu, zǒu dào hónglǜdēng yòu zhuǎn, bǐ zǒu zhè tiáo xiàngzi kuài duōle.
Right — keep going straight, turn right at the traffic light. It's much faster than walking through this alley.

[Turn 4 — 承翰 looks at his watch, worried]
承翰：太好了，那我大概幾分鐘可以到？我已經遲到十分鐘了。
Tài hǎo le, nà wǒ dàgài jǐ fēnzhōng kěyǐ dào? Wǒ yǐjīng chídào shí fēnzhōng le.
Great — so about how many minutes until I get there? I'm already ten minutes late.

[Turn 5 — 婉婷 gives an encouraging smile]
婉婷：別擔心，你跑步過去，五分鐘應該就到了，加油！
Bié dānxīn, nǐ pǎobù guòqù, wǔ fēnzhōng yīnggāi jiù dào le, jiāyóu!
Don't worry — run there, and you should arrive in about five minutes. Good luck!

[Turn 6 — 承翰 thanks her and runs off toward the station]
Narrator：承翰謝謝婉婷，然後跑去捷運站，最後比平常更快到學校，他很開心。
Chénghàn xièxie Wǎntíng, ránhòu pǎo qù jiéyùnzhàn, zuìhòu bǐ píngcháng gèng kuài dào xuéxiào, tā hěn kāixīn.
Chenghan thanks Wanting, then runs to the MRT station; in the end he arrives at school faster than usual, and he's very happy.

Key Phrases
1  從來沒 + V過      —  他從來沒走過這條路。            —  Say you've never done something before (experience negation).
2  是不是要...？     —  去捷運站是不是要一直往前走？    —  Politely confirm a plan or method (A-not-A with 是不是).
3  比 + N + Vs + 多了/更 —  比走這條巷子快多了。／比平常更快到學校。 —  Compare two things directly with 比.
