《我的錢包不見了》My Wallet Is Missing
Situation: A student can't find his wallet right before he's supposed to meet a friend, and his roommate helps him search the apartment.
Source: Lesson 5 – 我的錢包在哪裡？Where Is My Wallet? (Dialogue 2)

═══════════════════
EASY (A1)
═══════════════════

[Stage 1 — 客廳 The living room]
建宏：我的錢包呢？
Wǒ de qiánbāo ne?
Where's my wallet?

建宏：錢包不在這裡。
Qiánbāo bú zài zhèlǐ.
The wallet isn't here.

大偉：怎麼了？
Zěnme le?
What's wrong?

建宏：我找不到我的錢包。
Wǒ zhǎo bú dào wǒ de qiánbāo.
I can't find my wallet.

大偉：你要做什麼？
Nǐ yào zuò shénme?
What are you going to do?

建宏：我要跟朋友喝下午茶。
Wǒ yào gēn péngyǒu hē xiàwǔchá.
I'm going to have afternoon tea with a friend.

[Stage 2 — 找錢包 Looking around the apartment]
大偉：桌子上面有嗎？
Zhuōzi shàngmiàn yǒu ma?
Is it on the table?

建宏：沒有，桌子上面沒有。
Méiyǒu, zhuōzi shàngmiàn méiyǒu.
No, it's not on the table.

大偉：沙發下面呢？
Shāfā xiàmiàn ne?
What about under the sofa?

建宏：沙發下面也沒有。
Shāfā xiàmiàn yě méiyǒu.
Not under the sofa either.

大偉：廚房裡有嗎？
Chúfáng lǐ yǒu ma?
Is it in the kitchen?

建宏：廚房裡也沒有。
Chúfáng lǐ yě méiyǒu.
Not in the kitchen either.

[Stage 3 — 房間 His room]
建宏：我去房間找找。
Wǒ qù fángjiān zhǎozhǎo.
I'll go look in my room.

建宏：啊！在這裡！
À! Zài zhèlǐ!
Ah! It's here!

建宏：錢包在書的下面。
Qiánbāo zài shū de xiàmiàn.
The wallet is under the book.

大偉：太好了！
Tài hǎo le!
Great!

建宏：我們走吧！
Wǒmen zǒu ba!
Let's go!

大偉：好，走吧！
Hǎo, zǒu ba!
Okay, let's go!

Vocabulary
1  錢包   qiánbāo   N    wallet
2  在     zài       Vst  to be located at
3  哪裡   nǎlǐ      N    where
4  找     zhǎo      V    to look for
5  房間   fángjiān  N    room
6  客廳   kètīng    N    living room
7  桌子   zhuōzi    N    table
8  沙發   shāfā     N    sofa
9  廚房   chúfáng   N    kitchen
10 上面   shàngmiàn N    above; on top of
11 下面   xiàmiàn   N    below; underneath
12 書     shū       N    book
13 朋友   péngyǒu   N    friend
14 下午茶 xiàwǔchá  N    afternoon tea

Key Phrases
1  X呢？        —  我的錢包呢？          —  Ask "where's X?" briefly, no verb needed.
2  V不到         —  我找不到我的錢包。    —  Say you tried to do something but couldn't manage it.
3  地方也沒有    —  廚房裡也沒有。        —  Check another place and say "not there either" with 也.

═══════════════════
MEDIUM (A1-A2)
═══════════════════

[Stage 1 — 客廳 The living room]
建宏：糟糕，我的錢包不見了！
Zāogāo, wǒ de qiánbāo bújiàn le!
Oh no, my wallet is gone!

大偉：怎麼了？你在找什麼？
Zěnme le? Nǐ zài zhǎo shénme?
What's wrong? What are you looking for?

建宏：我在找我的錢包，可是找不到。
Wǒ zài zhǎo wǒ de qiánbāo, kěshì zhǎo bú dào.
I'm looking for my wallet, but I can't find it.

大偉：你今天要做什麼？
Nǐ jīntiān yào zuò shénme?
What are you doing today?

建宏：我下午兩點要跟朋友去喝下午茶，可是沒有錢包不能去。
Wǒ xiàwǔ liǎng diǎn yào gēn péngyǒu qù hē xiàwǔchá, kěshì méiyǒu qiánbāo bù néng qù.
I'm meeting a friend for afternoon tea at 2pm, but I can't go without my wallet.

大偉：別擔心，我陪你一起找。
Bié dānxīn, wǒ péi nǐ yìqǐ zhǎo.
Don't worry, I'll help you look.

[Stage 2 — 找錢包 Looking around the apartment]
大偉：客廳的桌子上面有嗎？
Kètīng de zhuōzi shàngmiàn yǒu ma?
Is it on the living room table?

建宏：桌子上面沒有，桌子下面也沒有。
Zhuōzi shàngmiàn méiyǒu, zhuōzi xiàmiàn yě méiyǒu.
Not on the table, and not under it either.

大偉：沙發上面、下面、旁邊呢？
Shāfā shàngmiàn, xiàmiàn, pángbiān ne?
What about on, under, or beside the sofa?

建宏：沙發那裡都沒有，真奇怪。
Shāfā nàlǐ dōu méiyǒu, zhēn qíguài.
Nothing around the sofa either — that's really strange.

大偉：廚房裡呢？你今天早上有沒有去廚房？
Chúfáng lǐ ne? Nǐ jīntiān zǎoshàng yǒu méiyǒu qù chúfáng?
What about the kitchen? Did you go there this morning?

建宏：我今天早上沒有去廚房。
Wǒ jīntiān zǎoshàng méiyǒu qù chúfáng.
I didn't go to the kitchen this morning.

[Stage 3 — 房間 His room]
建宏：我再去房間看看。
Wǒ zài qù fángjiān kànkàn.
I'll go check my room again.

建宏：啊，找到了！錢包在我的書下面！
À, zhǎodào le! Qiánbāo zài wǒ de shū xiàmiàn!
Ah, found it! The wallet is under my book!

大偉：太好了，你可以出門了。
Tài hǎo le, nǐ kěyǐ chūmén le.
Great, now you can leave.

建宏：謝謝你幫我找錢包。
Xièxiè nǐ bāng wǒ zhǎo qiánbāo.
Thanks for helping me find my wallet.

大偉：不客氣，你快去吧，別遲到了。
Bú kèqì, nǐ kuài qù ba, bié chídào le.
You're welcome, go quickly, don't be late.

建宏：好，我現在就走！
Hǎo, wǒ xiànzài jiù zǒu!
Okay, I'm leaving right now!

Vocabulary
15 不見了 bújiàn le  (phrase) gone; missing
16 擔心   dānxīn     V/Vs to worry
17 陪     péi        V    to accompany
18 旁邊   pángbiān   N    beside; next to
19 奇怪   qíguài     Vs   strange; odd
20 出門   chūmén     Vsep to go out; to leave the house
21 遲到   chídào     Vsep to be late
22 可是   kěshì      Conj but; however

Key Phrases
1  在 + V + 什麼？   —  你在找什麼？                    —  Ask what someone is currently doing (progressive action).
2  可是 + problem     —  可是沒有錢包不能去。            —  Contrast a plan with a problem using 可是.
3  V + 有沒有 + V     —  你今天早上有沒有去廚房？        —  Ask yes/no about a past action with 有沒有 + verb.

═══════════════════
HARD (A2)
═══════════════════

[Stage 1 — 客廳 The living room]
建宏：糟糕！我今天早上明明把錢包放在桌子上，現在怎麼不見了？
Zāogāo! Wǒ jīntiān zǎoshàng míngmíng bǎ qiánbāo fàng zài zhuōzi shàng, xiànzài zěnme bújiàn le?
Oh no! I clearly put my wallet on the table this morning — how is it gone now?

大偉：你先別緊張，我們一起找找看，說不定是你自己忘記放到哪裡去了。
Nǐ xiān bié jǐnzhāng, wǒmen yìqǐ zhǎozhǎokàn, shuōbúdìng shì nǐ zìjǐ wàngjì fàngdào nǎlǐ qù le.
Don't panic yet, let's look together — maybe you just forgot where you put it.

建宏：可是我已經找了半天，客廳、房間都找過了，還是沒看到。
Kěshì wǒ yǐjīng zhǎole bàntiān, kètīng, fángjiān dōu zhǎoguò le, háishì méi kàndào.
But I've already been looking for ages — I've checked the living room and my room, and still haven't seen it.

大偉：你等一下要出門嗎？
Nǐ děng yíxià yào chūmén ma?
Do you need to go out soon?

建宏：對啊，我跟朋友約好下午兩點半喝下午茶，已經比平常晚一點了。
Duì a, wǒ gēn péngyǒu yuēhǎo xiàwǔ liǎng diǎn bàn hē xiàwǔchá, yǐjīng bǐ píngcháng wǎn yìdiǎn le.
Yeah, I arranged to meet a friend for afternoon tea at 2:30 — I'm already a bit later than usual.

大偉：那我們動作要快一點。
Nà wǒmen dòngzuò yào kuài yìdiǎn.
Then we'd better move a bit faster.

[Stage 2 — 找錢包 Looking around the apartment]
大偉：你確定桌子上面、下面都看過了嗎？
Nǐ quèdìng zhuōzi shàngmiàn, xiàmiàn dōu kànguò le ma?
Are you sure you checked both on and under the table?

建宏：看過了，比我想的還仔細，連椅子旁邊都找了。
Kànguò le, bǐ wǒ xiǎng de hái zǐxì, lián yǐzi pángbiān dōu zhǎo le.
I checked, even more carefully than I expected — I even looked beside the chairs.

大偉：那沙發呢？有時候東西會滑到沙發跟牆壁中間。
Nà shāfā ne? Yǒu shíhòu dōngxī huì huádào shāfā gēn qiángbì zhōngjiān.
What about the sofa? Sometimes things slide down between the sofa and the wall.

建宏：沙發我也翻過了，什麼都沒有。
Shāfā wǒ yě fānguò le, shénme dōu méiyǒu.
I dug through the sofa too, nothing there.

大偉：奇怪，那會不會在廚房？你今天早上是不是在那裡吃過早餐？
Qíguài, nà huì bú huì zài chúfáng? Nǐ jīntiān zǎoshàng shì bú shì zài nàlǐ chīguò zǎocān?
Strange, could it be in the kitchen? Didn't you have breakfast there this morning?

建宏：對，不過我記得吃完就回房間了，應該不在廚房。
Duì, búguò wǒ jìdé chīwán jiù huí fángjiān le, yīnggāi bú zài chúfáng.
Right, but I remember going back to my room right after eating, so it shouldn't be in the kitchen.

[Stage 3 — 房間 His room]
建宏：等等，我想起來了，我昨天晚上看書看到一半就睡著了！
Děngděng, wǒ xiǎngqǐlái le, wǒ zuótiān wǎnshàng kànshū kàndào yíbàn jiù shuìzháo le!
Wait, I just remembered — last night I fell asleep halfway through reading!

建宏：我去房間看看書桌。
Wǒ qù fángjiān kànkàn shūzhuō.
I'll go check my desk in my room.

建宏：找到了！錢包一直壓在我看到一半的那本書下面。
Zhǎodào le! Qiánbāo yìzhí yāzài wǒ kàndào yíbàn de nà běn shū xiàmiàn.
Found it! The wallet was under the book I fell asleep reading the whole time.

大偉：你看，比你想的簡單多了。
Nǐ kàn, bǐ nǐ xiǎng de jiǎndān duō le.
See, that was a lot simpler than you thought.

建宏：謝謝你陪我找了這麼久，不然我今天一定會遲到。
Xièxiè nǐ péi wǒ zhǎole zhème jiǔ, bùrán wǒ jīntiān yídìng huì chídào.
Thanks for searching with me for so long — otherwise I definitely would've been late today.

大偉：不客氣，快走吧，不然真的要遲到了！
Bú kèqì, kuài zǒu ba, bùrán zhēnde yào chídào le!
No problem, go quickly, or you really will be late!

Vocabulary
23 糟糕     zāogāo      Vs   (interj.) oh no; how terrible
24 明明     míngmíng    Adv  clearly; obviously
25 說不定   shuōbúdìng  Adv  maybe; perhaps
26 忘記     wàngjì      V    to forget
27 確定     quèdìng     V    to be sure; to confirm
28 仔細     zǐxì        Vs   careful; attentive
29 牆壁     qiángbì     N    wall
30 應該     yīnggāi     Aux  should; ought to
31 睡著     shuìzháo    V-comp to fall asleep
32 不然     bùrán       Conj otherwise
33 簡單     jiǎndān     Vs   simple

Key Phrases
1  把 + N + V在 + place  —  我把錢包放在桌子上。          —  State where you put something using the 把 construction.
2  地方 + 都 + V過了      —  客廳、房間都找過了。          —  Say you've already searched/done something in every place mentioned.
3  比 + 想的 + Vs + 多了  —  比你想的簡單多了。            —  Compare reality to expectation: "much more X than expected."
