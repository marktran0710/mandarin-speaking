《我的房間和我的貓》My Room and My Cat
Situation: A friend visits for the first time and gets a tour of the host's room — his books, his favorite reading spot, and the cat that naps by the window.
Source: Lesson 5 – 我的錢包在哪裡？Where Is My Wallet? (Reading: 我的房間 / My Room)

═══════════════════
EASY (A1)
═══════════════════

[Turn 1 — 中明家門口，下午，友美剛到，中明帶她走進房間]
Narrator：友美來中明家。他們一起去房間。
Yǒuměi lái Zhōngmíng jiā. Tāmen yìqǐ qù fángjiān.
Youmei comes to Zhongming's house. They go to his room together.

[Turn 2 — 房間裡，友美看著桌子上一疊一疊的書]
友美：哇！你的書好多！
Wa! Nǐ de shū hǎo duō!
Wow! You have so many books!

[Turn 3 — 中明指著沙發，笑著說]
中明：我喜歡在沙發上看書。
Wǒ xǐhuān zài shāfā shàng kànshū.
I like reading on the sofa.

[Turn 4 — 友美看到窗戶旁邊有一隻貓，很驚喜]
友美：那是你的貓嗎？好可愛！
Nà shì nǐ de māo ma? Hǎo kě'ài!
Is that your cat? So cute!

[Turn 5 — 中明微笑，指著貓]
中明：牠叫橘子。牠喜歡曬太陽。
Tā jiào Júzi. Tā xǐhuān shài tàiyáng.
Its name is Juzi. It likes basking in the sun.

[Turn 6 — 友美蹲下來，開心地笑著看貓]
友美：我也想要一隻貓！
Wǒ yě xiǎng yào yì zhī māo!
I want a cat too!

Vocabulary
1  書      shū        N    book
2  好多     hǎoduō     (pattern) so many; a lot of
3  喜歡     xǐhuān     V    to like
4  沙發     shāfā      N    sofa
5  窗戶     chuānghù   N    window
6  貓      māo        N    cat
7  可愛     kě'ài      Vs   cute; adorable
8  曬太陽    shài tàiyáng VO  to bask in the sun
9  隻      zhī        M    measure word for animals
10 想要     xiǎngyào   V    to want

Key Phrases
1  X 好多！        —  你的書好多！          —  Exclaim there's a lot of something, 好 + quantity word for emphasis.
2  喜歡 + V         —  我喜歡在沙發上看書。   —  Say what activity someone likes, verb goes right after 喜歡.
3  那是...嗎？      —  那是你的貓嗎？         —  Ask to confirm the identity of something you see.

═══════════════════
MEDIUM (A1-A2)
═══════════════════

[Turn 1 — 中明家門口，下午，友美剛到，中明帶她走進房間]
Narrator：友美今天下午來中明家玩，中明帶她去看他的房間。
Yǒuměi jīntiān xiàwǔ lái Zhōngmíng jiā wán, Zhōngmíng dài tā qù kàn tā de fángjiān.
Youmei comes over to Zhongming's place this afternoon, and he takes her to see his room.

[Turn 2 — 房間裡，友美看著桌子、床跟沙發，覺得很舒服]
友美：你的房間裡有桌子、床，還有沙發，真舒服！
Nǐ de fángjiān lǐ yǒu zhuōzi, chuáng, hái yǒu shāfā, zhēn shūfú!
Your room has a table, a bed, and a sofa too — so comfortable!

[Turn 3 — 中明指著桌上的書，再指指沙發]
中明：桌子上有很多書，可是我最喜歡在沙發上看書。
Zhuōzi shàng yǒu hěn duō shū, kěshì wǒ zuì xǐhuān zài shāfā shàng kànshū.
There are lots of books on the table, but I like reading on the sofa the most.

[Turn 4 — 友美看到窗戶旁邊有一隻貓在睡覺，很驚喜]
友美：窗戶旁邊那隻貓是你的嗎？牠好可愛！
Chuānghù pángbiān nà zhī māo shì nǐ de ma? Tā hǎo kě'ài!
Is that cat next to the window yours? It's so cute!

[Turn 5 — 中明微笑，指著貓]
中明：對啊，牠叫橘子，因為喜歡曬太陽，所以常常在窗戶旁邊睡覺。
Duì a, tā jiào Júzi, yīnwèi xǐhuān shài tàiyáng, suǒyǐ chángcháng zài chuānghù pángbiān shuìjiào.
Yeah, its name is Juzi — because it loves basking in the sun, it often sleeps by the window.

[Turn 6 — 友美蹲下來摸貓，開心地笑]
友美：太可愛了，我也好想養一隻貓！
Tài kě'ài le, wǒ yě hǎo xiǎng yǎng yì zhī māo!
So cute, I really want to have a cat too!

Vocabulary
11 舒服        shūfú          Vs   comfortable
12 最          zuì            Adv  most
13 因為...所以... yīnwèi...suǒyǐ... Conj because...so...
14 常常        chángcháng     Adv  often
15 睡覺        shuìjiào       VO   to sleep
16 養          yǎng           V    to raise; to keep (a pet)
17 對啊        duì a          (phrase) yeah, that's right

Key Phrases
1  因為...，所以...  —  因為喜歡曬太陽，所以常常在窗戶旁邊睡覺。  —  Give a reason and its result with 因為...所以....
2  最 + V             —  我最喜歡在沙發上看書。                    —  Say which activity you like most, 最 goes right before the verb phrase.
3  太...了            —  太可愛了！                                —  React strongly and positively: "so/too X!"

═══════════════════
HARD (A2)
═══════════════════

[Turn 1 — 中明家門口，下午，友美剛到，中明一開門就帶她走進房間]
Narrator：友美這個週末下午來中明家玩，他一開門就帶她去看他剛整理過的房間。
Yǒuměi zhège zhōumò xiàwǔ lái Zhōngmíng jiā wán, tā yì kāimén jiù dài tā qù kàn tā gāng zhěnglǐguò de fángjiān.
Youmei comes over to Zhongming's place this weekend afternoon, and the moment he opens the door, he takes her to see the room he just tidied up.

[Turn 2 — 房間裡，友美看著桌子、床跟沙發，覺得比想像的舒服]
友美：你的房間比我想的乾淨多了，還有桌子、床跟沙發呢！
Nǐ de fángjiān bǐ wǒ xiǎng de gānjìng duō le, hái yǒu zhuōzi, chuáng gēn shāfā ne!
Your room is a lot cleaner than I expected, and it's even got a table, a bed, and a sofa!

[Turn 3 — 中明指著桌上的書，再指指沙發]
中明：桌上的書我大概都看過了，不過我最常坐在沙發上看。
Zhuō shàng de shū wǒ dàgài dōu kànguò le, búguò wǒ zuì cháng zuò zài shāfā shàng kàn.
I've pretty much read all the books on the table, but I usually sit on the sofa to read.

[Turn 4 — 友美看到窗戶旁邊的貓睡得很熟，很驚喜]
友美：窗戶旁邊那隻貓睡得比我還舒服，牠是你養的嗎？
Chuānghù pángbiān nà zhī māo shuì de bǐ wǒ hái shūfú, tā shì nǐ yǎng de ma?
That cat by the window is sleeping more comfortably than I do — is it yours?

[Turn 5 — 中明微笑，摸摸貓的毛]
中明：對啊，牠叫橘子，因為每天都在那裡曬太陽，毛已經比以前更軟了。
Duì a, tā jiào Júzi, yīnwèi měitiān dōu zài nàlǐ shài tàiyáng, máo yǐjīng bǐ yǐqián gèng ruǎn le.
Yeah, its name is Juzi — because it basks in the sun there every day, its fur has gotten even softer than before.

[Turn 6 — 友美蹲下來摸貓，露出羨慕的表情]
友美：真羨慕，我也好想養一隻，可是我家不能養寵物。
Zhēn xiànmù, wǒ yě hǎo xiǎng yǎng yì zhī, kěshì wǒ jiā bù néng yǎng chǒngwù.
I'm so jealous, I really want to have one too, but my place doesn't allow pets.

Vocabulary
18 大概   dàgài    Adv  roughly; probably
19 不過   búguò    Conj but; however
20 毛     máo      N    fur
21 軟     ruǎn     Vs   soft
22 羨慕   xiànmù   V    to envy
23 寵物   chǒngwù  N    pet
24 更     gèng     Adv  even more

Key Phrases
1  比 + 我想的 + Vs + 多了  —  你的房間比我想的乾淨多了。      —  Compare something to your expectation: much more X than you thought.
2  V過了                    —  桌上的書我大概都看過了。          —  Say you've already done/experienced something, using 過 as an aspect marker.
3  比 + N + 還 + Vs         —  那隻貓睡得比我還舒服。            —  Compare two things directly, "more X than Y," using 還 for emphasis.
