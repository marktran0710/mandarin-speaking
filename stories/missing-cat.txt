《咪咪不見了》Mimi Is Missing
Situation: A roommate is about to leave for class when he realizes the family cat is missing, and his roommate helps him search the apartment room by room.
Source: Lesson 5 – 我的錢包在哪裡？Where Is My Wallet? (Dialogue 2)

═══════════════════
EASY (A1)
═══════════════════

[Turn 1 — 客廳 The living room, morning; 志偉 has his backpack on, looking at the empty sofa]
Narrator：志偉要去上課。沙發上沒有咪咪。
Zhìwěi yào qù shàngkè. Shāfā shàng méiyǒu Mīmī.
Zhiwei needs to go to class. Mimi isn't on the sofa.

[Turn 2 — 志偉 calls out to his roommate 佩珍]
志偉：佩珍，妳看到咪咪嗎？
Pèizhēn, nǐ kàndào Mīmī ma?
Peizhen, do you see Mimi?

[Turn 3 — 佩珍 points toward the table]
佩珍：桌子下面有嗎？
Zhuōzi xiàmiàn yǒu ma?
Is she under the table?

[Turn 4 — 志偉 crouches down, checks, shakes his head]
志偉：沒有，桌子下面沒有。
Méiyǒu, zhuōzi xiàmiàn méiyǒu.
No, she's not under the table.

[Turn 5 — 佩珍 points toward the bedroom door]
佩珍：房間裡呢？
Fángjiān lǐ ne?
What about in the bedroom?

[Turn 6 — 志偉 kneels by the bed, finds Mimi curled up asleep, smiles]
Narrator：志偉找到咪咪。他很高興。
Zhìwěi zhǎodào Mīmī. Tā hěn gāoxìng.
Zhiwei finds Mimi. He is happy.

Vocabulary
1  要      yào        Aux   want to; need to
2  去      qù         V     to go
3  上課    shàngkè    Vsep  to attend class
4  沙發    shāfā      N     sofa
5  上面    shàngmiàn  N     above; on top of
6  沒有    méiyǒu     V/Adv there isn't; not to have
7  貓      māo        N     cat
8  看到    kàndào     V-comp to see; to catch sight of
9  嗎      ma         Ptc   question particle
10 桌子    zhuōzi     N     table
11 下面    xiàmiàn    N     below; underneath
12 有      yǒu        Vst   there is/are; to have
13 房間    fángjiān   N     room
14 裡      lǐ         N     inside; in
15 呢      ne         Ptc   "what about...?" particle
16 找到    zhǎodào    V-comp to find
17 高興    gāoxìng    Vs    happy

Key Phrases
1  地方 + 呢？        —  房間裡呢？          —  Suggest checking another place, "what about X?"
2  V + 到 (resultative) —  妳看到咪咪嗎？／志偉找到咪咪。 —  Attach 到 to a verb to show the action succeeded (saw/found).
3  沒有                —  桌子下面沒有。      —  Say something isn't there using 沒有 on its own.

═══════════════════
MEDIUM (A1-A2)
═══════════════════

[Turn 1 — 客廳 The living room, morning; 志偉 checks the empty sofa, a little anxious]
Narrator：今天志偉要去上課，可是他找不到貓咪咪，客廳的沙發上也沒有牠。
Jīntiān Zhìwěi yào qù shàngkè, kěshì tā zhǎo bú dào māo Mīmī, kètīng de shāfā shàng yě méiyǒu tā.
Today Zhiwei needs to go to class, but he can't find the cat Mimi — she's not on the living room sofa either.

[Turn 2 — 志偉 calls toward the kitchen where 佩珍 is]
志偉：佩珍，妳有沒有看到咪咪？我沒有很多時間。
Pèizhēn, nǐ yǒu méiyǒu kàndào Mīmī? Wǒ méiyǒu hěn duō shíjiān.
Peizhen, have you seen Mimi? I don't have much time.

[Turn 3 — 佩珍 gestures toward the table and sofa]
佩珍：你可以先看看客廳的桌子下面跟沙發旁邊。
Nǐ kěyǐ xiān kànkan kètīng de zhuōzi xiàmiàn gēn shāfā pángbiān.
You can first check under the living room table and beside the sofa.

[Turn 4 — 志偉 checks both spots, shrugs]
志偉：桌子下面沒有，沙發旁邊也沒有，真奇怪。
Zhuōzi xiàmiàn méiyǒu, shāfā pángbiān yě méiyǒu, zhēn qíguài.
Not under the table, and not beside the sofa either — that's really strange.

[Turn 5 — 佩珍 gestures toward the bedroom door]
佩珍：因為你早上開窗戶，牠可能在房間裡。
Yīnwèi nǐ zǎoshàng kāi chuānghù, tā kěnéng zài fángjiān lǐ.
Because you opened the window this morning, she's probably in the bedroom.

[Turn 6 — 志偉 kneels by the bed, finds Mimi, smiles with relief]
Narrator：志偉去房間找，咪咪就在床下面，他終於放心。
Zhìwěi qù fángjiān zhǎo, Mīmī jiù zài chuáng xiàmiàn, tā zhōngyú fàngxīn.
Zhiwei goes to look in the bedroom; Mimi is right there under the bed — he finally feels relieved.

Vocabulary
18 今天    jīntiān    N    today
19 可是    kěshì      Conj but
20 客廳    kètīng     N    living room
21 也      yě         Adv  also; too
22 時間    shíjiān    N    time
23 可以    kěyǐ       Aux  can; may
24 先      xiān       Adv  first
25 跟      gēn        Conj/Prep and; with
26 旁邊    pángbiān   N    beside; next to
27 真      zhēn       Adv  really; truly
28 奇怪    qíguài     Vs   strange; odd
29 因為    yīnwèi     Conj because
30 早上    zǎoshàng   N    morning
31 開      kāi        V    to open
32 窗戶    chuānghù   N    window
33 可能    kěnéng     Adv  maybe; possibly
34 終於    zhōngyú    Adv  finally
35 放心    fàngxīn    Vs/V to feel at ease; relieved
36 床      chuáng     N    bed

Key Phrases
1  有沒有 + V         —  妳有沒有看到咪咪？                 —  Ask a yes/no question about a past action with 有沒有 + verb.
2  因為 X，Y           —  因為你早上開窗戶，牠可能在房間裡。 —  Give a reason before a possible result.
3  可以 + 先 + V       —  你可以先看看客廳的桌子下面。       —  Suggest someone do something first.

═══════════════════
HARD (A2)
═══════════════════

[Turn 1 — 客廳 The living room, morning; 志偉 rushes around, checking the empty sofa anxiously]
Narrator：志偉今天起晚了，出門前才發現貓咪咪不見了，客廳的沙發上完全沒有牠的蹤影。
Zhìwěi jīntiān qǐ wǎn le, chūmén qián cái fāxiàn māo Mīmī bújiàn le, kètīng de shāfā shàng wánquán méiyǒu tā de zōngyǐng.
Zhiwei got up late today; right before leaving he discovered the cat Mimi was missing — there wasn't a trace of her on the living room sofa at all.

[Turn 2 — 志偉 calls urgently toward the kitchen]
志偉：佩珍，妳今天早上是不是有看到咪咪？我已經找了十分鐘了。
Pèizhēn, nǐ jīntiān zǎoshàng shì bú shì yǒu kàndào Mīmī? Wǒ yǐjīng zhǎole shí fēnzhōng le.
Peizhen, did you happen to see Mimi this morning? I've already been searching for ten minutes.

[Turn 3 — 佩珍 comes over, thinking back]
佩珍：我記得早上開窗戶的時候她還在客廳，你確定桌子下面跟沙發旁邊都看過了嗎？
Wǒ jìdé zǎoshàng kāi chuānghù de shíhòu tā hái zài kètīng, nǐ quèdìng zhuōzi xiàmiàn gēn shāfā pángbiān dōu kànguò le ma?
I remember she was still in the living room when I opened the window this morning — are you sure you've checked under the table and beside the sofa?

[Turn 4 — 志偉 gestures, a little frustrated]
志偉：看過了，比我想的還仔細，連窗簾後面都找了，可是還是沒看到。
Kànguò le, bǐ wǒ xiǎngde hái zǐxì, lián chuānglián hòumiàn dōu zhǎo le, kěshì háishì méi kàndào.
I checked — even more carefully than I expected, I even looked behind the curtains — but I still haven't found her.

[Turn 5 — 佩珍 gestures toward the bedroom door, thinking]
佩珍：那會不會躲到房間裡去了？貓有時候會找一個安靜的地方睡覺。
Nà huì bú huì duǒdào fángjiān lǐ qù le? Māo yǒushíhòu huì zhǎo yí ge ānjìng de dìfāng shuìjiào.
Could she have hidden away in the bedroom? Cats sometimes look for a quiet place to sleep.

[Turn 6 — 志偉 rushes to the bedroom, kneels by the bed, finds Mimi asleep, relieved smile]
Narrator：志偉衝去房間，發現咪咪正安靜地睡在床下面，比他想的還可愛，他終於放心地出門了。
Zhìwěi chōng qù fángjiān, fāxiàn Mīmī zhèng ānjìng de shuì zài chuáng xiàmiàn, bǐ tā xiǎngde hái kěài, tā zhōngyú fàngxīn de chūmén le.
Zhiwei rushes to the bedroom and discovers Mimi quietly sleeping under the bed — even cuter than he expected — he finally leaves the house feeling relieved.

Vocabulary
37 起晚    qǐ wǎn      V-comp to get up late
38 出門    chūmén      Vsep   to go out; to leave the house
39 才      cái         Adv    only then; not until
40 發現    fāxiàn      V      to discover
41 不見了  bújiàn le   Ph     gone; missing
42 完全    wánquán     Adv    completely
43 蹤影    zōngyǐng    N      trace; sign (of someone/something)
44 記得    jìdé        V      to remember
45 確定    quèdìng     V      to be sure; to confirm
46 仔細    zǐxì        Vs     careful; attentive
47 窗簾    chuānglián  N      curtain
48 後面    hòumiàn     N      behind; at the back
49 還是    háishì      Adv    still
50 躲      duǒ         V      to hide
51 有時候  yǒushíhòu   Adv    sometimes
52 安靜    ānjìng      Vs     quiet
53 地方    dìfāng      N      place
54 睡覺    shuìjiào    V      to sleep

Key Phrases
1  是不是 + V...？     —  妳今天早上是不是有看到咪咪？        —  Politely confirm something happened (A-not-A with 是不是).
2  已經 + V + 了       —  我已經找了十分鐘了。               —  Mark how long you've already been doing something.
3  比 + X + 還 + Vs    —  比我想的還仔細。／比他想的還可愛。 —  Compare reality to expectation, "even more X than."
