《朋友要來我們家》Friends Are Coming to Our House
Situation: Two roommates race to tidy the living room and get snacks ready in the kitchen before friends arrive for a visit.
Source: Lesson 5 – 我的錢包在哪裡？Where Is My Wallet? (Dialogue 2)

═══════════════════
EASY (A1)
═══════════════════

[Stage 1 — 客廳 The living room]
家豪：詩涵，你在哪裡？
Shīhán, nǐ zài nǎlǐ?
Shihan, where are you?

詩涵：我在房間。
Wǒ zài fángjiān.
I'm in my room.

家豪：中明跟友美要來我們家。
Zhōngmíng gēn Yǒuměi yào lái wǒmen jiā.
Zhongming and Youmei are coming to our place.

詩涵：太好了！我們去客廳吧。
Tài hǎo le! Wǒmen qù kètīng ba.
Great! Let's go to the living room.

家豪：客廳有一張桌子跟兩張椅子。
Kètīng yǒu yì zhāng zhuōzi gēn liǎng zhāng yǐzi.
The living room has one table and two chairs.

詩涵：沙發在桌子旁邊。
Shāfā zài zhuōzi pángbiān.
The sofa is next to the table.

家豪：好，客廳很漂亮。
Hǎo, kètīng hěn piàoliang.
Good, the living room looks nice.

[Stage 2 — 廚房 The kitchen]
家豪：我們去廚房吧。
Wǒmen qù chúfáng ba.
Let's go to the kitchen.

詩涵：廚房裡有很多水果。
Chúfáng lǐ yǒu hěn duō shuǐguǒ.
There's a lot of fruit in the kitchen.

家豪：那是哥哥買的。
Nà shì gēge mǎi de.
That was bought by my brother.

詩涵：桌子上面也有果汁。
Zhuōzi shàngmiàn yě yǒu guǒzhī.
There's also juice on the table.

家豪：我們拿水果去客廳吧。
Wǒmen ná shuǐguǒ qù kètīng ba.
Let's take the fruit to the living room.

詩涵：好，我拿果汁。
Hǎo, wǒ ná guǒzhī.
Okay, I'll carry the juice.

[Stage 3 — 客人到了 The guests arrive]
中明：你好！我們到了。
Nǐ hǎo! Wǒmen dào le.
Hi! We're here.

家豪：你們好，請進。
Nǐmen hǎo, qǐng jìn.
Hello, please come in.

友美：你們家很漂亮！
Nǐmen jiā hěn piàoliang!
Your home is very nice!

詩涵：謝謝，沙發在這裡，你們坐吧。
Xièxiè, shāfā zài zhèlǐ, nǐmen zuò ba.
Thanks, the sofa is here, please sit.

中明：客廳有水果跟果汁，太好了！
Kètīng yǒu shuǐguǒ gēn guǒzhī, tài hǎo le!
The living room has fruit and juice, great!

家豪：我們一起吃吧！
Wǒmen yìqǐ chī ba!
Let's eat together!

Vocabulary
1  房間   fángjiān   N     room
2  客廳   kètīng     N     living room
3  桌子   zhuōzi     N     table
4  椅子   yǐzi       N     chair
5  張     zhāng      M     measure word for tables/chairs
6  沙發   shāfā      N     sofa
7  旁邊   pángbiān   N     next to; beside
8  廚房   chúfáng    N     kitchen
9  水果   shuǐguǒ    N     fruit
10 果汁   guǒzhī     N     juice
11 哥哥   gēge       N     older brother
12 拿     ná         V     to take; to carry
13 漂亮   piàoliang  Vs    pretty; nice-looking
14 請進   qǐngjìn    phrase please come in
15 跟     gēn        Conj  and

Key Phrases
1  我們 + V + 吧   —  我們去客廳吧。          —  Suggest doing something together, "let's...".
2  地方 + 有 + N   —  客廳有一張桌子跟兩張椅子。 —  Say what exists in a place using 有.
3  N + 在 + 地方   —  沙發在桌子旁邊。        —  State where something is located with 在.

═══════════════════
MEDIUM (A1-A2)
═══════════════════

[Stage 1 — 客廳 The living room]
家豪：詩涵，中明跟友美晚上七點要來我們家。
Shīhán, Zhōngmíng gēn Yǒuměi wǎnshàng qī diǎn yào lái wǒmen jiā.
Shihan, Zhongming and Youmei are coming to our place at 7pm tonight.

詩涵：現在才五點，可是客廳還很亂。
Xiànzài cái wǔ diǎn, kěshì kètīng hái hěn luàn.
It's only 5 now, but the living room is still messy.

家豪：桌子在房間裡，我們一起搬過來吧。
Zhuōzi zài fángjiān lǐ, wǒmen yìqǐ bānguòlái ba.
The table is in the bedroom, let's carry it over together.

詩涵：好，因為朋友要來，所以我們要快一點。
Hǎo, yīnwèi péngyǒu yào lái, suǒyǐ wǒmen yào kuài yìdiǎn.
Okay, since friends are coming, we need to hurry a bit.

家豪：桌子放在沙發前面，椅子放在桌子旁邊，怎麼樣？
Zhuōzi fàngzài shāfā qiánmiàn, yǐzi fàngzài zhuōzi pángbiān, zěnmeyàng?
The table goes in front of the sofa, chairs beside the table — how's that?

詩涵：很好，客廳現在舒服多了。
Hěn hǎo, kètīng xiànzài shūfú duō le.
Great, the living room is much more comfortable now.

[Stage 2 — 廚房 The kitchen]
詩涵：我去廚房拿水果跟果汁。
Wǒ qù chúfáng ná shuǐguǒ gēn guǒzhī.
I'll go to the kitchen to get fruit and juice.

家豪：冰箱裡的水果是誰買的？
Bīngxiāng lǐ de shuǐguǒ shì shéi mǎi de?
Whose fruit is that in the fridge?

詩涵：是哥哥昨天買的，他也買了一些餅乾。
Shì gēge zuótiān mǎi de, tā yě mǎile yìxiē bǐnggān.
My brother bought it yesterday, he also bought some cookies.

家豪：太好了，我最喜歡餅乾配果汁。
Tài hǎo le, wǒ zuì xǐhuān bǐnggān pèi guǒzhī.
Great, I like cookies with juice the most.

詩涵：那你先拿餐具去客廳，我去洗水果。
Nà nǐ xiān ná cānjù qù kètīng, wǒ qù xǐ shuǐguǒ.
Then you take the dishes to the living room first, I'll go wash the fruit.

家豪：好，你需要我幫忙嗎？
Hǎo, nǐ xūyào wǒ bāngmáng ma?
Okay, do you need my help?

詩涵：不用，你去客廳等朋友就好。
Búyòng, nǐ qù kètīng děng péngyǒu jiù hǎo.
No need, you just go wait for our friends in the living room.

[Stage 3 — 客人到了 The guests arrive]
中明：詩涵、家豪，我們到了！
Shīhán, Jiāháo, wǒmen dào le!
Shihan, Jiahao, we're here!

家豪：來了來了，你們吃飯了嗎？
Lái le lái le, nǐmen chīfàn le ma?
Coming, coming — have you guys eaten?

友美：還沒，我們肚子有點餓。
Hái méi, wǒmen dùzi yǒudiǎn è.
Not yet, we're a little hungry.

詩涵：太好了，廚房有水果，客廳的桌子上也有餅乾。
Tài hǎo le, chúfáng yǒu shuǐguǒ, kètīng de zhuōzi shàng yě yǒu bǐnggān.
Great, there's fruit in the kitchen, and cookies on the living room table too.

中明：你們的新家真舒服，沙發也很好坐。
Nǐmen de xīn jiā zhēn shūfú, shāfā yě hěn hǎo zuò.
Your new place is really comfortable, the sofa is nice to sit on too.

家豪：謝謝，我們坐下來一起聊天吧。
Xièxiè, wǒmen zuòxiàlái yìqǐ liáotiān ba.
Thanks, let's sit down and chat together.

Vocabulary
16 可是   kěshì      Conj  but
17 因為   yīnwèi     Conj  because
18 所以   suǒyǐ      Conj  so; therefore
19 才     cái        Adv   only then; not until
20 亂     luàn       Vs    messy
21 先     xiān       Adv   first
22 一起   yìqǐ       Adv   together
23 放     fàng       V     to put; to place
24 前面   qiánmiàn   N     in front of
25 舒服   shūfú      Vs    comfortable
26 冰箱   bīngxiāng  N     refrigerator
27 昨天   zuótiān    N     yesterday
28 餅乾   bǐnggān    N     cookie
29 最     zuì        Adv   most
30 喜歡   xǐhuān     V     to like
31 幫忙   bāngmáng   V     to help
32 肚子   dùzi       N     stomach
33 餓     è          Vs    hungry
34 聊天   liáotiān   V     to chat

Key Phrases
1  因為...，所以...   —  因為朋友要來，所以我們要快一點。 —  Give a reason, then its result.
2  Vs + 多了           —  客廳現在舒服多了。               —  Say something is "much more X now" (comparative degree).
3  需要 + V + 嗎？     —  你需要我幫忙嗎？                 —  Politely ask if someone needs something/help.

═══════════════════
HARD (A2)
═══════════════════

[Stage 1 — 客廳 The living room]
家豪：詩涵，我剛剛看了一下時間，中明他們大概八點才會到，我們應該還來得及整理。
Shīhán, wǒ gānggāng kànle yíxià shíjiān, Zhōngmíng tāmen dàgài bā diǎn cái huì dào, wǒmen yīnggāi hái láidejí zhěnglǐ.
Shihan, I just checked the time — Zhongming and the others probably won't get here until around 8, so we should still have time to tidy up.

詩涵：太好了，不過客廳比昨天亂多了，我們得先收拾一下。
Tài hǎo le, búguò kètīng bǐ zuótiān luàn duō le, wǒmen děi xiān shōushí yíxià.
Great, but the living room is way messier than yesterday, we need to tidy up a bit first.

家豪：桌子跟椅子都還在房間裡，沒搬出來，我們動作要快一點。
Zhuōzi gēn yǐzi dōu hái zài fángjiān lǐ, méi bānchūlái, wǒmen dòngzuò yào kuài yìdiǎn.
The table and chairs are still in the bedroom, they haven't been moved out — we need to move faster.

詩涵：好，我們一起搬，這樣比較快。
Hǎo, wǒmen yìqǐ bān, zhèyàng bǐjiào kuài.
Okay, let's carry them together, that'll be faster.

家豪：桌子放在沙發前面，看起來比剛剛整齊多了。
Zhuōzi fàngzài shāfā qiánmiàn, kànqǐlái bǐ gānggāng zhěngqí duō le.
The table's in front of the sofa now, it looks much tidier than before.

詩涵：對啊，客廳整理過以後感覺完全不一樣。
Duì a, kètīng zhěnglǐguò yǐhòu gǎnjué wánquán bù yíyàng.
Right, the living room feels completely different after being tidied up.

[Stage 2 — 廚房 The kitchen]
家豪：我去廚房看看有什麼吃的，你先休息一下吧。
Wǒ qù chúfáng kànkàn yǒu shénme chī de, nǐ xiān xiūxí yíxià ba.
I'll go check what there is to eat in the kitchen, you rest for a bit first.

詩涵：好，冰箱裡應該還有哥哥昨天買的水果，你可以拿出來切一切。
Hǎo, bīngxiāng lǐ yīnggāi hái yǒu gēge zuótiān mǎi de shuǐguǒ, nǐ kěyǐ ná chūlái qiè yí qiè.
Okay, there should still be the fruit my brother bought yesterday in the fridge, you can take it out and cut it up.

家豪：找到了，比我想的還多，看起來夠我們四個人吃了。
Zhǎodào le, bǐ wǒ xiǎngde hái duō, kànqǐlái gòu wǒmen sì ge rén chī le.
Found it — there's more than I expected, looks like enough for the four of us.

詩涵：太好了，那餅乾跟果汁也一起放到客廳的桌子上吧。
Tài hǎo le, nà bǐnggān gēn guǒzhī yě yìqǐ fàngdào kètīng de zhuōzi shàng ba.
Great, then let's put the cookies and juice on the living room table too.

家豪：沒問題，我等一下都準備好，妳去換件衣服吧。
Méi wèntí, wǒ děng yíxià dōu zhǔnbèi hǎo, nǐ qù huàn jiàn yīfú ba.
No problem, I'll get everything ready in a bit, you go change your clothes.

詩涵：好，謝謝你，我五分鐘就好。
Hǎo, xièxiè nǐ, wǒ wǔ fēnzhōng jiù hǎo.
Okay, thanks, I'll be five minutes.

[Stage 3 — 客人到了 The guests arrive]
中明：家豪，不好意思，我們可能比原來說的晚了十分鐘。
Jiāháo, bùhǎoyìsi, wǒmen kěnéng bǐ yuánlái shuō de wǎnle shí fēnzhōng.
Jiahao, sorry, we might be about ten minutes later than we originally said.

家豪：沒關係，你們來得剛剛好，我們也是五分鐘前才準備好的。
Méi guānxi, nǐmen láide gānggānghǎo, wǒmen yě shì wǔ fēnzhōng qián cái zhǔnbèihǎo de.
No worries, your timing's perfect — we only just finished getting ready five minutes ago ourselves.

友美：哇，你們的客廳整理得比上次乾淨多了！
Wa, nǐmen de kètīng zhěnglǐ de bǐ shàngcì gānjìng duō le!
Wow, your living room is so much cleaner than last time!

詩涵：謝謝，我們今天下午花了快兩個小時整理。
Xièxiè, wǒmen jīntiān xiàwǔ huāle kuài liǎng ge xiǎoshí zhěnglǐ.
Thanks, we spent almost two hours tidying up this afternoon.

中明：辛苦你們了，那我們趕快坐下來，先吃點水果吧。
Xīnkǔ nǐmen le, nà wǒmen gǎnkuài zuòxiàlái, xiān chī diǎn shuǐguǒ ba.
That's a lot of work — let's sit down quickly and have some fruit first then.

家豪：來，大家隨便坐，今天晚上我們可以聊到很晚。
Lái, dàjiā suíbiàn zuò, jīntiān wǎnshàng wǒmen kěyǐ liáodào hěn wǎn.
Come on, everyone sit wherever, we can chat until late tonight.

Key Phrases
1  比 + N + Vs + 多了     —  客廳比昨天亂多了。／比上次乾淨多了！ —  Compare against a specific reference point using 比.
2  V + 過以後，...        —  客廳整理過以後感覺完全不一樣。       —  Describe a change that happens after completing an action.
3  V得 + Adv              —  你們來得剛剛好。                     —  Describe how well/precisely an action was done (manner complement with 得).
