《我們去喝下午茶》Let's Get Afternoon Tea
Situation: A student texts a friend to make weekend plans, invites her for afternoon tea at a café famous for its ice cream, and they agree on a time to meet.
Source: Lesson 5 – 我的錢包在哪裡？Where Is My Wallet? (Dialogue 1)

═══════════════════
EASY (A1)
═══════════════════

[Turn 1 — 中明在家，晚上，拿著手機傳訊息給友美]
中明：友美，妳週末要做什麼？
Yǒuměi, nǐ zhōumò yào zuò shénme?
Youmei, what are you doing this weekend?

[Turn 2 — 友美坐在床上，手裡拿著書，旁邊有耳機]
友美：我要在家看書、聽音樂。
Wǒ yào zài jiā kànshū, tīng yīnyuè.
I'm going to stay home, read, and listen to music.

[Turn 3 — 中明繼續打字，臉上帶著期待的表情]
中明：妳星期六下午有空嗎？我們喝下午茶，好嗎？
Nǐ xīngqíliù xiàwǔ yǒukòng ma? Wǒmen hē xiàwǔchá, hǎo ma?
Are you free Saturday afternoon? Let's have afternoon tea, okay?

[Turn 4 — 友美微笑看著手機，開心地回覆]
友美：好啊！去哪裡？
Hǎo a! Qù nǎlǐ?
Sure! Where should we go?

[Turn 5 — 中明指著手機裡一家咖啡廳的照片]
中明：那家咖啡廳不錯，有巧克力冰淇淋。
Nà jiā kāfēitīng búcuò, yǒu qiǎokèlì bīngqílín.
That café is good — they have chocolate ice cream.

[Turn 6 — 兩人一起坐在咖啡廳，桌上有兩杯冰淇淋]
友美：我們星期六下午兩點半去吧。
Wǒmen xīngqíliù xiàwǔ liǎng diǎn bàn qù ba.
Let's go this Saturday at 2:30 in the afternoon.

Vocabulary
1  在      zài        Vst/Prep  (to be located) at; in; on
2  哪裡    nǎlǐ       N   where
3  聽      tīng       V   to listen
4  音樂    yīnyuè     N   music
5  有空    yǒukòng    Vs  free; available
6  下午茶  xiàwǔchá   N   afternoon tea
7  不錯    búcuò      Vs  not bad; pretty good
8  咖啡廳  kāfēitīng  N   café
9  冰淇淋  bīngqílín  N   ice cream
10 巧克力  qiǎokèlì   N   chocolate
11 半      bàn        N   half
12 吧      ba         Ptc final particle for suggestion

Key Phrases
1  X 有空嗎？        —  妳星期六下午有空嗎？   —  Ask if someone is free before inviting them somewhere.
2  在 + 地方 + V      —  我要在家看書、聽音樂。 —  Say where an action happens, with 在 before the place.
3  ...吧             —  我們兩點半去吧。       —  Add 吧 to soften a suggestion into "let's..."

═══════════════════
MEDIUM (A1-A2)
═══════════════════

[Turn 1 — 中明在家，晚上，拿著手機傳訊息給友美]
中明：友美，妳這個週末想做什麼？
Yǒuměi, nǐ zhège zhōumò xiǎng zuò shénme?
Youmei, what do you want to do this weekend?

[Turn 2 — 友美坐在床上，手裡拿著書，旁邊有耳機]
友美：這個週末我想在家看書、聽音樂，好好休息。
Zhège zhōumò wǒ xiǎng zài jiā kànshū, tīng yīnyuè, hǎohǎo xiūxí.
This weekend I want to stay home, read, listen to music, and rest well.

[Turn 3 — 中明繼續打字，臉上帶著期待的表情]
中明：妳星期六下午有空嗎？我們一起去喝下午茶，怎麼樣？
Nǐ xīngqíliù xiàwǔ yǒukòng ma? Wǒmen yìqǐ qù hē xiàwǔchá, zěnmeyàng?
Are you free Saturday afternoon? How about we go have afternoon tea together?

[Turn 4 — 友美微笑看著手機，開心地回覆]
友美：好啊，可是要去哪裡呢？你想去哪裡？
Hǎo a, kěshì yào qù nǎlǐ ne? Nǐ xiǎng qù nǎlǐ?
Sure, but where should we go? Where do you want to go?

[Turn 5 — 中明指著手機裡一家咖啡廳的照片]
中明：我知道一家咖啡廳很不錯，那裡的巧克力冰淇淋特別好吃。
Wǒ zhīdào yì jiā kāfēitīng hěn búcuò, nàlǐ de qiǎokèlì bīngqílín tèbié hǎochī.
I know a café that's quite good — their chocolate ice cream is especially delicious.

[Turn 6 — 兩人一起坐在咖啡廳，桌上有兩杯冰淇淋]
友美：太好了，我們這個星期六下午兩點半去那裡吧！
Tài hǎo le, wǒmen zhège xīngqíliù xiàwǔ liǎng diǎn bàn qù nàlǐ ba!
Great, let's go there this Saturday at 2:30 in the afternoon!

Vocabulary
13 那裡    nàlǐ       N     there
14 特別    tèbié      Adv   especially; particularly
15 可是    kěshì      Conj  but; however
16 怎麼樣  zěnmeyàng  QW    how about it? how is it?
17 太...了 tài...le   (pattern) too/so...!

Key Phrases
1  A，可是 B          —  好啊，可是要去哪裡呢？          —  Agree to something while raising a related question or concern.
2  ...，怎麼樣？      —  我們一起去喝下午茶，怎麼樣？    —  Propose an idea and invite the other person's reaction.
3  太...了            —  太好了！                        —  React strongly and positively to good news.

═══════════════════
HARD (A2)
═══════════════════

[Turn 1 — 中明在家，晚上，拿著手機傳訊息給友美]
中明：友美，我們好久沒見面了，妳這個週末要做什麼？
Yǒuměi, wǒmen hǎojiǔ méi jiànmiàn le, nǐ zhège zhōumò yào zuò shénme?
Youmei, we haven't seen each other in a while — what are you doing this weekend?

[Turn 2 — 友美坐在床上，手裡拿著書，旁邊有耳機]
友美：我這幾天有點累，想在家看書、聽音樂，好好休息一下。
Wǒ zhè jǐ tiān yǒudiǎn lèi, xiǎng zài jiā kànshū, tīng yīnyuè, hǎohǎo xiūxí yíxià.
I've been a bit tired these past few days, so I want to stay home, read, listen to music, and rest a while.

[Turn 3 — 中明繼續打字，臉上帶著期待的表情]
中明：那妳星期六下午有空嗎？要不要一起去喝杯下午茶，放鬆一下？
Nà nǐ xīngqíliù xiàwǔ yǒukòng ma? Yào bú yào yìqǐ qù hē bēi xiàwǔchá, fàngsōng yíxià?
Then are you free Saturday afternoon? Want to go have some afternoon tea together and relax a bit?

[Turn 4 — 友美微笑看著手機，開心地回覆]
友美：好啊，聽起來比在家看書有趣多了，我們去哪裡呢？
Hǎo a, tīng qǐlái bǐ zài jiā kànshū yǒuqù duō le, wǒmen qù nǎlǐ ne?
Sure, that sounds a lot more fun than staying home reading — where should we go?

[Turn 5 — 中明指著手機裡一家咖啡廳的照片]
中明：我上次去過一家咖啡廳，那裡的冰淇淋比別家好吃很多，還有各種口味的巧克力冰淇淋。
Wǒ shàngcì qùguò yì jiā kāfēitīng, nàlǐ de bīngqílín bǐ biéjiā hǎochī hěn duō, hái yǒu gèzhǒng kǒuwèi de qiǎokèlì bīngqílín.
I went to a café last time — their ice cream is much better than other places, and they have all kinds of chocolate ice cream flavors.

[Turn 6 — 兩人一起坐在咖啡廳，桌上有兩杯冰淇淋]
友美：太棒了，那我們這個星期六下午兩點半就約在那裡吧！
Tài bàng le, nà wǒmen zhège xīngqíliù xiàwǔ liǎng diǎn bàn jiù yuē zài nàlǐ ba!
Awesome, then let's meet there this Saturday at 2:30 in the afternoon!

Vocabulary
18 好久不見  hǎojiǔ bújiàn (phrase) long time no see
19 放鬆      fàngsōng      V   to relax
20 過        guò           Ptc aspect marker: have done (experience)
21 比        bǐ            Prep than (comparison)
22 有趣      yǒuqù         Vs  interesting; fun
23 各種      gèzhǒng       Det all kinds of; various
24 口味      kǒuwèi        N   flavor; taste
25 約        yuē           V   to arrange to meet
26 棒        bàng          Vs  great; awesome

Key Phrases
1  A 比 B + Vs + 多了   —  聽起來比在家看書有趣多了。       —  Compare two things, saying one is clearly more X than the other.
2  V 過                 —  我上次去過一家咖啡廳。           —  Mention having done something before, as a past experience.
3  好久沒 V 了          —  我們好久沒見面了。               —  Note it's been a long time since doing/seeing something.
