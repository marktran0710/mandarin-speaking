《我們去士林夜市》Let's Go to Shilin Night Market
Situation: A friend texts another to make weekend plans, inviting her to Shilin Night Market for fried chicken and bubble tea, and they agree on a time and place to meet.
Source: Lesson 5 – 我的錢包在哪裡？Where Is My Wallet? (Dialogue 1), mode: generate

═══════════════════
EASY (A1)
═══════════════════

[Turn 1 — 志豪在家，星期五晚上，滑著手機，想找佳玲出去]
Narrator：星期五晚上，志豪在家，想找佳玲一起出去。
Xīngqíwǔ wǎnshàng, Zhìháo zài jiā, xiǎng zhǎo Jiālíng yìqǐ chūqù.
Friday evening, Zhihao is home, wanting to ask Jialing to go out together.

[Turn 2 — 志豪打字傳訊息，臉上帶著期待的表情]
志豪：佳玲，妳星期六晚上要做什麼？
Jiālíng, nǐ xīngqíliù wǎnshàng yào zuò shénme?
Jialing, what are you doing Saturday evening?

[Turn 3 — 佳玲坐在沙發上，拿著手機微笑回覆]
佳玲：我沒事，你呢？
Wǒ méishì, nǐ ne?
I'm free, what about you?

[Turn 4 — 志豪傳來士林夜市的照片，興奮地提出邀請]
志豪：我們去士林夜市，好嗎？那裡的雞排很好吃。
Wǒmen qù Shìlín yèshì, hǎo ma? Nàlǐ de jīpái hěn hǎochī.
Let's go to Shilin Night Market, okay? The fried chicken there is delicious.

[Turn 5 — 佳玲眼睛發亮，開心地打字回覆]
佳玲：太好了！那裡有珍珠奶茶嗎？
Tài hǎo le! Nàlǐ yǒu zhēnzhū nǎichá ma?
Great! Do they have bubble tea there?

[Turn 6 — 志豪比出「六」的手勢，笑著確認見面時間和地點]
志豪：有啊！我們六點在捷運站見。
Yǒu a! Wǒmen liù diǎn zài jiéyùnzhàn jiàn.
Yes! Let's meet at the MRT station at six.

Vocabulary
1  沒事      méishì          Vs  free; nothing planned
2  呢        ne              Ptc question particle: "and...?"/"what about...?"
3  夜市      yèshì           N   night market
4  雞排      jīpái           N   fried chicken cutlet
5  好吃      hǎochī          Vs  delicious
6  太好了    tài hǎo le      Ph  great!; wonderful!
7  珍珠奶茶  zhēnzhū nǎichá  N   bubble tea
8  捷運站    jiéyùnzhàn      N   MRT station
9  見        jiàn            V   to meet; to see

Key Phrases
1  ...，好嗎？     —  我們去士林夜市，好嗎？    —  Propose a plan and check if the other person is okay with it.
2  那裡有 X 嗎？   —  那裡有珍珠奶茶嗎？        —  Ask whether a specific place has something you want.
3  我們[time]在[place]見。 —  我們六點在捷運站見。 —  Fix a time and place to meet someone.

MEDIUM (A1-A2)
═══════════════════

[Turn 1 — 志豪在家，星期五晚上，滑著手機，想找佳玲出去]
Narrator：星期五晚上，志豪在家，想找佳玲這個週末一起出去玩。
Xīngqíwǔ wǎnshàng, Zhìháo zài jiā, xiǎng zhǎo Jiālíng zhège zhōumò yìqǐ chūqù wán.
Friday evening, Zhihao is home, wanting to ask Jialing to go out and have fun together this weekend.

[Turn 2 — 志豪打字傳訊息，臉上帶著期待的表情]
志豪：佳玲，這個星期六晚上妳想做什麼？
Jiālíng, zhège xīngqíliù wǎnshàng nǐ xiǎng zuò shénme?
Jialing, what do you want to do this Saturday evening?

[Turn 3 — 佳玲坐在沙發上，拿著手機微笑回覆]
佳玲：我還沒有計畫，怎麼了？
Wǒ hái méiyǒu jìhuà, zěnme le?
I don't have any plans yet — why do you ask?

[Turn 4 — 志豪傳來士林夜市的照片，興奮地提出邀請]
志豪：我們一起去士林夜市，好不好？那裡有一家雞排特別好吃。
Wǒmen yìqǐ qù Shìlín yèshì, hǎo bù hǎo? Nàlǐ yǒu yì jiā jīpái tèbié hǎochī.
Let's go to Shilin Night Market together, okay? There's a fried chicken stall there that's especially delicious.

[Turn 5 — 佳玲眼睛發亮，開心地打字回覆]
佳玲：好啊，可是我更想喝珍珠奶茶，那裡有嗎？
Hǎo a, kěshì wǒ gèng xiǎng hē zhēnzhū nǎichá, nàlǐ yǒu ma?
Sure, but I want bubble tea even more — do they have that there?

[Turn 6 — 志豪比出「六」的手勢，笑著確認見面時間和地點]
志豪：當然有！我們星期六晚上六點在捷運站門口見面吧。
Dāngrán yǒu! Wǒmen xīngqíliù wǎnshàng liù diǎn zài jiéyùnzhàn ménkǒu jiànmiàn ba.
Of course! Let's meet at the MRT station entrance at six on Saturday evening.

Vocabulary
10 計畫      jìhuà       N    plan
11 還沒(有)  hái méi(yǒu) Ph  not yet
12 怎麼了    zěnme le    Ph   what's wrong?; why (do you ask)?
13 好不好    hǎo bù hǎo  Ph   is that okay? (tag question)
14 特別      tèbié       Adv  especially
15 可是      kěshì       Conj but; however
16 更        gèng        Adv  even more
17 當然      dāngrán     Adv  of course
18 門口      ménkǒu      N    entrance; doorway

Key Phrases
1  A，好不好？     —  我們一起去士林夜市，好不好？   —  Propose a plan and invite the other person's agreement.
2  A，可是 B       —  好啊，可是我更想喝珍珠奶茶。   —  Agree to something while adding a preference or concern.
3  還沒(有) X       —  我還沒有計畫。                 —  Say you haven't done or decided something yet.

HARD (A2)
═══════════════════

[Turn 1 — 志豪在家，星期五晚上，滑著手機，想找佳玲出去]
Narrator：星期五晚上，志豪在家滑手機，突然想到已經好幾個星期沒跟佳玲出去了。
Xīngqíwǔ wǎnshàng, Zhìháo zài jiā huá shǒujī, túrán xiǎngdào yǐjīng hǎo jǐ ge xīngqí méi gēn Jiālíng chūqù le.
Friday evening, Zhihao is at home scrolling his phone when he suddenly realizes it's been several weeks since he went out with Jialing.

[Turn 2 — 志豪打字傳訊息，臉上帶著期待的表情]
志豪：佳玲，我們好久沒見面了，妳這個星期六晚上有空嗎？
Jiālíng, wǒmen hǎojiǔ méi jiànmiàn le, nǐ zhège xīngqíliù wǎnshàng yǒukòng ma?
Jialing, it's been so long since we've seen each other — are you free this Saturday evening?

[Turn 3 — 佳玲坐在沙發上，拿著手機微笑回覆]
佳玲：剛好有空，我最近工作有點累，想出去放鬆一下。
Gānghǎo yǒukòng, wǒ zuìjìn gōngzuò yǒudiǎn lèi, xiǎng chūqù fàngsōng yíxià.
I happen to be free — I've been a bit tired from work lately and want to go out and relax a little.

[Turn 4 — 志豪傳來士林夜市的照片，興奮地提出邀請]
志豪：那我們一起去士林夜市吧，我上次去過，那裡的雞排比別家好吃很多。
Nà wǒmen yìqǐ qù Shìlín yèshì ba, wǒ shàngcì qùguò, nàlǐ de jīpái bǐ biéjiā hǎochī hěn duō.
Then let's go to Shilin Night Market together — I went last time, and the fried chicken there is much better than other places.

[Turn 5 — 佳玲眼睛發亮，開心地打字回覆]
佳玲：聽起來不錯，我也想喝那裡有名的珍珠奶茶。
Tīng qǐlái búcuò, wǒ yě xiǎng hē nàlǐ yǒumíng de zhēnzhū nǎichá.
That sounds great — I also want to drink the famous bubble tea there.

[Turn 6 — 志豪比出「六」的手勢，笑著確認見面時間和地點]
志豪：沒問題，我們星期六晚上六點在捷運站門口見面，比平常早一點出發。
Méi wèntí, wǒmen xīngqíliù wǎnshàng liù diǎn zài jiéyùnzhàn ménkǒu jiànmiàn, bǐ píngcháng zǎo yìdiǎn chūfā.
No problem — let's meet at the MRT station entrance at six on Saturday evening; we'll set off a bit earlier than usual.

Vocabulary
19 好久沒...了  hǎojiǔ méi...le  Ph   haven't done X in a long time
20 剛好         gānghǎo          Adv  it happens that; coincidentally
21 有點         yǒudiǎn          Adv  a little; somewhat
22 放鬆         fàngsōng         V    to relax
23 上次         shàngcì          N    last time
24 過           guò              Ptc  aspect marker: have done (experience)
25 比           bǐ               Prep than (comparison)
26 聽起來       tīngqǐlái        Ph   it sounds like...
27 有名         yǒumíng          Vs   famous
28 平常         píngcháng        N/Adv usually; normally

Key Phrases
1  好久沒 V 了       —  我們好久沒見面了。               —  Note it's been a long time since doing or seeing something.
2  A 比 B + Vs + 多了  —  那裡的雞排比別家好吃很多。       —  Compare two things directly, saying one clearly beats the other.
3  V 過               —  我上次去過。                     —  Mention having done something before, as a past experience.
